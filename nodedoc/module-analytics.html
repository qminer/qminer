Cost parameter. Increasing the parameter forces the model to fit the training data more accurately (setting it too large may lead to overfitting)

svcParam.j

number

Yes

Unbalance parameter. Increasing it gives more weight to the positive examples (getting a better fit on the positive training examples gets a higher priority). Setting c=n is like adding n-1 copies of the positive training examples to the data set.

Defaults to 1.0.
